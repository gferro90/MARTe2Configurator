Detailed Description

A collector of functions that implements logical and mathematical shift in safe mode.

These methods implements shift operations in the right way, returning zero when the shift is greater than the size of the number. Furthermore they implements the logical shift which does not extend the sign for negative numbers and it is very useful in many functionalities.
